The simple idea

So… what is iConnect?

Getting older is hard — more health problems, more medicines, more help needed, and more loneliness. Today, all of that is handled by different people using systems that don't talk to each other. Nobody has the full picture.

iConnect fixes that by putting everything in one place. It's a brand, a piece of software, a wearable device, and a network of trained humans — all acting as one team around each older person.

01

A Brand

A trusted name organizations, communities and caregivers operate under — a badge of quality care for the elderly.

02

The Software + AI

The brain. It holds every patient's history, automates the boring work, tracks meds and meals, and routes emergencies.

03

The Device

A wearable worn 24/7 by every patient and every caregiver. It reads vitals, senses trouble, and stays connected.

04

The People

Specialist doctors, trained nurses, and caregivers who complete intense iConnect training — the human heart of it all.

The story, step by step

How iConnect works

Five simple moves take someone from "just turned 75" to "wrapped in a complete care network."

01the trigger

🎂You turn 75 → iConnect activates

The moment a person turns 75, their existing health records (their "MyChart" health history) are pulled into iConnect and reorganized into one clean, complete profile. From this day, iConnect has the full medical picture in a single place.

Their whole history, converted
02the wearable

⌚A device for everyone — worn 24/7

Every patient over 75 wears an iConnect device around the clock. So does every caregiver. The device continuously reads vitals, tracks daily activity, senses falls or distress, and never disconnects.

Patient + caregiver, both wearing
03the safety net

🚨Everyone important is linked in

The device doesn't just reach the caregiver. It's wired directly to the emergency world — 911, the fire department, and disaster relief services. If something goes wrong, the right help is alerted automatically and instantly.

Help is one heartbeat away
04the humans

🧑‍⚕️Trained specialists provide the care

iConnect care is delivered by geriatricians (doctors for older adults), geriatric & gerontological nurses, and caregivers who complete intense professional training through iConnect. Each caregiver looks after a manageable group of 10–15 patients.

Real, qualified, supported people
05the key idea

📁The care follows the patient — not the person

A patient's history is never locked to one caregiver or doctor. It lives in the software, the AI agent, and the device. If a caregiver leaves, goes on holiday, or the patient switches doctors — nothing is lost. The next professional instantly has the whole story. Care never breaks.

Data moves freely, care stays constant

Who actually uses iConnect?

iConnect is built to serve three different kinds of care providers — each with their own way in.

Full members

Organizations

Senior communities & care facilities

Organizations operate under the iConnect name and get the complete platform — every program, club, and service, plus the full software and device system for their residents.

  • Run all iConnect social & wellness programs
  • Full brand, software, and device access
  • Complete care coordination for every resident
Their level of access
The entire iConnect world — clubs, programs, health services & specialists, all in-house.
Independent

Professional caregivers

Working on their own, outside an organization

Caregivers who want to operate separately — not under an organization's roof — still belong to iConnect. They keep their independence but get a standing invitation to the facility.

  • Keep running their own independent practice
  • Full access to the iConnect software & devices
  • Bring their patients to the iConnect facility
Their facility access
Bring patients in once a month — a regular touchpoint with the full facility.
At home

Home caregivers

Family & at-home carers

People caring for a loved one at home aren't left out. They can bring the person under their care to an iConnect facility as a visitor — flexibly, whenever it's needed.

  • Care for a loved one in their own home
  • Visit the facility whenever the need arises
  • Access iConnect's specialists & programs as a guest
Their facility access
Visit whenever needed, and by pay — flexible, on-demand support.

A day in the life — and the moment it matters

Meet Eleanor. Step through her day to see every part of iConnect working together — from her morning coffee to a heart-stopping emergency, and safely out the other side.

👵

Eleanor Hartwell, 76

Lives alone in a quiet neighbourhood. Mild heart condition, allergic to penicillin, takes 3 daily medications. Loves her garden and her Tuesday laughter club. Her caregiver, Marcus, looks after 12 people — Eleanor is one of them.

6:47 AM — the morning

The day begins, quietly watched over

Eleanor wakes and makes coffee. Her iConnect device has been reading her vitals all night. Everything looks normal — but her blood pressure is a touch high, so the system gently notes it and flags it for Marcus to glance at later. Her 8 AM medication reminder softly buzzes on her wrist.

👵Eleanor

Wakes, makes coffee, feels fine. Gets a gentle nudge for her morning pills.

📟Device

Logged vitals all night. Notes slightly high BP. Fires med reminder.

🧑‍⚕️Marcus

Sees Eleanor's "green" status on his dashboard, with a small BP note.

🤖iConnect

Silently monitoring. Nothing needs escalating — yet.

8:30 AM — breakfast

A meal planned around her body

Breakfast follows Eleanor's dietary plan — heart-healthy, low sodium. The system already knows her penicillin allergy and her food preferences. Marcus's app tracked the ingredients, sourced fresh from a local farmer tie-up, and the grocery inventory ticked down automatically.

What it's really trying to achieve

Six goals at the heart of iConnect

The world is aging, and today's elderly-care system is fragmented, stressful and reactive. iConnect wants to make it connected, calm and proactive. Here's what it's really going after.

🛡️

Safety

24/7 wearables tied straight to 911, fire and disaster relief mean help arrives fast. No one falls through the cracks.

🔗

Continuity of care

Records live in the software, not with one person — so care never breaks when caregivers or doctors change.

📈

Better health outcomes

Automated meds, meal plans, allergy notes and diets mean fewer mistakes and healthier patients.

🤗

Fighting loneliness

Clubs, laughter therapy, hobbies and outings keep older adults connected — and give them purpose.

🧑‍⚕️

Supporting caregivers

Training, tools, financial clarity and mental-health support let caregivers do great work without burning out.

🤖

Technology as a helper

AI handles the repetitive work — scheduling, inventory, routing — so humans can focus on real, caring moments.
